How do I open a new account?

Opening an account is quick and easy. Download our mobile app or visit our website, provide basic personal details (name, phone number, BVN), and complete the verification process. You’ll get instant access to your new account. For assistance, contact our Customer Care team.

How can I check my account balance?

Check your balance anytime through our mobile app, USSD code (*followed by our code*), online banking portal, or at any ATM nationwide. Your balance is updated in real-time for complete peace of mind.

Is online and mobile banking secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication, biometric login, and constant monitoring to keep your account and transactions fully protected. Always avoid sharing your PIN or OTP with anyone.

What is the daily transfer limit?

Daily transfer limits vary by account type and verification level. You can view and manage your limits directly in the app under Settings > Security & Limits. Contact Customer Care if you need a limit increase.

How do I transfer money to other banks?

Transferring to other banks is instant and seamless. Simply log into the app or online banking, select “Transfer”, choose “To Other Banks”, enter the recipient’s account details, and confirm with your PIN or OTP. Funds arrive in seconds in most cases.

What should I do if I forget my PIN or password?

Use the “Forgot PIN/Password” option on the login screen. You’ll be guided through a secure reset process using your registered phone number or email. For further help, reach out to our 24/7 Customer Care team.
